VR Planetarium

Study the movement of constellations at different locations of earth

The project was done as part of the course titled Introduction to Serious Games. This application teaches the movement of stars and constellations across the night sky and the player gets a better understanding of how the stars move wrt time and location of the player. The player also learns about some geographical terms like azimuth and altitute that are eminent to the star's location. We are on root to making it into a treasure hunt puzzle game.

I teamed up with Terry Beyak and Nancy Lin on this project. This project was done using the Unity3D editor for Oculus platfrom. I worked on the time dial system, north axis of the earth, stars placement and player movement on earth.

Tools used: Unity3D, C#, Oculus Rift
