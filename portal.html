Portable ECG Device

Heart Rate Sensor on Raspberry Pi and Oscilloscope

This was a team project. This video shows you about the working of our new project called heart rate sensor. We have also published a research paper titled “A Portable Real Time ECG Device for Arrhythmia detection using Raspberry Pi”


I worked on designing the hardware part of the prototype. I also worked on the wireless data transfer of ECG data from Raspberry Pi to the Android app in the mobile device.


Tools used: EAGLE, Python, Raspberry Pi
