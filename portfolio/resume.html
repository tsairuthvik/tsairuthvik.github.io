AR Personal Resume

Augmented Reality based Personal Resume

I got the idea of this project when I was still building my personal website. Hence, I thought of building an augmented reality version of my personal website. I used my facebook profile pic as the image target and 9 cubes popped up when my photo was detected, these 9 cubes were associated with 9 different social media websites of mine like facebook, github, google scholar and my perosnal website too. I have also associated my Resume to a rectangular surface below. The websites can be opened by clicking on that particular cube.

Tools used: Vuforia AR SDK, Unity3D, C#
