Air Hockey PONG Version





The solo project was done as a part of the course titled Fundamentals of Game Engineering

Each player controls a "paddle" on one side of the screen. A ball in the middle starts flying towards one side of the screen. If the ball hits that player's paddle, the ball bounces off and flies towards the other side of the screen. If one player misses the ball with their paddle, the other player is awarded a point and a new ball spawns in the middle. In this Pong game, the field is changed to suit the air hockey version, an oscillating obstacle is added in the middle and additional pucks can be added to the game if the spacebar is pressed, so that the game gets difficult and exciting.

Tools used: C++, SFML, Visual Studio
