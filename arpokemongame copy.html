AR Pokemon Battle Game

Augmented Reality based Pokemon Battle Game on Cards

I have created a Marker-Based Augmented Reality version of the Pokemon Battle using Unity Editor, the Pokemon have only one attack each and have the same power level, hence the water Pokemon(Squirtle) will always win over the fire Pokemon(Charmander)(Pokemon Trivia). I will develop a proper Pokemon battle as we used to play in the GameBoy in the coming days.

Ball in a maze puzzle

Augmented Reality version of ball in a maze puzzle.

I have created a version of the famous puzzle "ball in a maze" in Augmented Reality using Unity Editor. This is Marker-based Augmented Reality with the maze image being the target and the virtual maze and the ball being the virtual objects tracked on the maze
