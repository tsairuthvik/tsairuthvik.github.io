The project was done as part of the course titled Game Design Systems, a text-based game on the health status of a crisis hotline operator. The operator must somehow keep his mental, physical and social health in balance in order to continue working, otherwise he is fired or he quits.

We were a team of three(I, Nick Banducci and Joe Huang). I worked primarily on developing the game on Twine and incorporating sound, health bar status updates using Javascript and CSS.

Tools used: Twine, HTML, CSS, Javascript
